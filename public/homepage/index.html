Introducing INSTANO

Instano is a virtual shopping platform that provides a common place for shoppers and retailers to close the best deal subject to customers preferences: pricing and proximity.

Discover Instano Get it on Google Play

How It WORKS

Instano lets you connect with the right store and negotiate quotations virtually with the dealer without any third-party interference.

  • Search Products
    Search for a product you seek to get a list of stores in your proximity.

  • Get Quotes
    Get real time pricing of the product offered by various stores nearby.

  • Go Shopping
    A lot of time is saved by finding the right shop offering the best deal.

Currently we are live only for consumer electronics stores. Stay tuned to get further updates. For an early access preview share your email with us.

The Features You'll Love

Instano comes with a lot of features to enhance your virtual shopping experience.

  • Location Based Shopping
    Get access to all the local stores in the area.

  • Multiple Products
    Sort out shops on the basis of products.

  • Brand Specific Searches
    Explore shops selling up a particular brand of interest.

  • Negotiate The Price
    Get quotes and negotiate the price with the retailer on the go.
